Lemony Garbanzo Salad

Description

A simple salad recipe with chickpeas and lemon

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2 cans chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp minced parsley
  • 1 tbsp orange marmalade
  • 4 cups spring mix salad greens

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together first 6 ingredients; stir in beans and green onions. In another bowl, mix yogurt, parsley and marmalade.
  2. To serve, divide greens among 4 plates; top with bean mixture. Serve with yogurt sauce.
